About Me

Elisha Faith D. Balaston is twenty one years old. She was born on June 1, 2003, in Malabon City. She currently lives in Arkong Bato, Valenzuela City. She is a student at City of Malabon University, studying for a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT), third year. She was a Dean’s Lister in the first semester school year 2022-2023. She also graduated from ROTC in 2023 at the same university.

She graduated with honors in Senior High School for grades 11 and 12 from Panghulo National High School from 2020 to 2022. Since Junior High School, she has enjoyed collecting certificates and achievements. She does this to make her parents proud because of her hard work in her studies.

She is the second of three siblings and comes from a supportive family. Her parents help her finish her education. She wants to complete her studies for her parents’ sacrifices, believing that one day she can give them a better life.
